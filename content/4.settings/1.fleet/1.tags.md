# Fleet Tags

## About
Fleet tags allow you to categorize and organize vehicles within your fleet. Tags help identify vehicle types, purposes, and assignments across the organization.

## How to Use Tags
Tags can be applied to individual vehicles or groups of vehicles to help manage your fleet more effectively.

## Common Tag Types
- **Department Tags**: Assign vehicles to specific departments (e.g., Highway Patrol, Fire & Rescue)
- **Status Tags**: Mark vehicles as active, maintenance, or reserved
- **Purpose Tags**: Identify vehicle roles (e.g., patrol, pursuit, emergency response)
- **Location Tags**: Track where vehicles are stationed

## Managing Tags
Use the fleet management system to add, edit, or remove tags from vehicles as needed.
