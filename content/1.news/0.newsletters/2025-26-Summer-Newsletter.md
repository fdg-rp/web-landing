## **Development Highlights**

Over the past few months the server as a whole has had a multitude of changes in the development scene.  I have been unable to list every major update as there have been so many, but I have included all of the biggest and best changes made by our Senior Staff & Development team over the Summer\!

### **🏪 Major Business Update \- Vespucci Markets🏪**   

**![VMarkets](https://imgur.com/XOi8I1G.png)**  

We introduced rentable shopfronts down at the Vespucci Markets, every player now has the chance to start a business with ease and sell products to others at any time of the day or week. 

All stores come fit with a configurable 24/7 shopfront that players can use to buy or sell items, this allows players to sell their items or buy what they need without the need of waiting for the owner to be online. Keeping in mind you pay a tax when there's no employee, for convenience\!

As you grow your business you can gain access to contracts (within BOD’s discretion) to allow long term progression and reward for effort. This allows players a chance to grow their business significantly if they put the effort in. 

**Huge shoutout** to Kn0pee, Bozzy, Dizabled and Lastonearth for the dev work on the markets.  


### **🔧Chop Shop🔧**  

![Chopshop title](https://imgur.com/K3080Tx.png)

A massive new criminal activity has also been launched recently, **Chop Shop**, the release came with a full overhaul of the outdated stolen car missions. Instead of the previous system, Chop Shop introduces a contract based loop. Players are able to take on car theft jobs with different levels of payouts, risks and difficulties with all decisions reflected in the players level in the levelling system. Chop Shop took the old system and re-did it entirely, making it more structured and rewarding.

Once players go find their vehicle and steal it they must go to a Chop Shop and dismantle it piece by piece and give them to Vlad. The better condition of the car the bigger the payout. The high profile contract system also brings more risk but alongside with more danger, by accepting players get paid more but police are sent an active location of their car. 

You can also have money making money with your friends with the co-op element which allows you and your friend to ride and dismantle cars together for the share of the payout.

### **🏆 Achievements & Quack Score🏆** 

![Achievements](https://imgur.com/ISCPTs3.png)

Another new introduction is the progression system seen in Achievements. 

Achievements are now in the server that reward players for simply engaging and playing, regardless of if you are a new player, casual player or long term player you are able to complete achievements and earn money and Quack Score (for obvious bragging rights).

It gives players new milestones to chase and as a way to show off their time on the server.

### **🔨Business Auctions🔨**

There has also been tons going on, on the server as well as just on the development side. A major event that occurred recently was the first ever FDG Business Auctions, the auctions allowed any player to bet on four separate businesses for sale. Instead of featuring on a tenure system where BOD maintains control of the business these auctions allowed for players to take full and total control and ownership.

The auctions were held for Weazel News, Bean Machine, Vanilla Unicorn and then finally Area 51, each auction started at $1 million and climbed based on the willingness of people to still bid.
