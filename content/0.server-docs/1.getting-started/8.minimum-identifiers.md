# Minimum Identifiers

To join the Fat Duck Gaming FiveM server, you must have a **minimum set of identifiers** linked to your FiveM/Cfx.re account. These identifiers help us verify who you are and keep the community safe.

You need **both** of the following:

::list{type="danger"}
- A **verified Discord account** linked to your FiveM client
- **At least one** of: Steam, Xbox / Microsoft Live, or a Cfx.re account
::

---

## Discord (Mandatory)

::alert{type="warning"}
Discord is **mandatory**. You must have Discord linked to your FiveM account **and** be verified in the FDG Discord server before you can join.
::

### Step 1 — Link Discord to FiveM

FiveM automatically detects and links your Discord identity when Discord is running on your computer at the same time as FiveM. Make sure Discord is open before you launch FiveM.

### Step 2 — Verify in the FDG Discord

You must also verify your Discord account inside the FDG Discord server. Follow the steps on the [How to Connect](/server-docs/getting-started/how-to-connect#discord-verification) page to complete verification.

---

## Steam, Xbox / Microsoft Live, or Cfx.re Account (At Least One Required)

In addition to Discord, you need **at least one** of the following platform accounts linked to your FiveM session.

### Steam

::alert{type="info"}
Make sure the **Steam client is open and you are logged in** before launching FiveM. FiveM detects your Steam identity automatically when Steam is running.
::

1. Open the **Steam** application and sign in to your account.
2. Launch **FiveM** — your Steam identity is automatically detected.

### Xbox / Microsoft Live

::alert{type="info"}
Make sure the **Xbox app (or Microsoft Store / Game Bar) is open and you are signed in** before launching FiveM.
::

1. Open the **Xbox** app (or sign in to your Microsoft account via the Xbox Game Bar — `WIN + G`).
2. Launch **FiveM** — your Xbox / Microsoft Live identity is automatically detected.

### Cfx.re Account

If you don't use Steam or Xbox, you can log in with a **Cfx.re account** directly inside FiveM.

1. Open **FiveM**.
2. On the FiveM main menu, click the **profile icon** or navigate to `Settings`.
3. Select **Sign in with Cfx.re** and follow the on-screen prompts to log in or create a free account at [forum.cfx.re](https://forum.cfx.re/).
4. Once signed in your Cfx.re identity will be linked automatically.
